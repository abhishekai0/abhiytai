How to use:

  1. Click the microphone button and allow microphone access when prompted
  2. Say "Hey Abhi, play [song name]" clearly in English
  3. The app will respond with voice feedback and play the song
  4. Use play/pause button to control playback
  5. Adjust volume using the slider
  6. Close the player by clicking the √ó button
  7. Say "Hey Abhi, add to playlist" to add current song to playlist
  8. Say "Hey Abhi, show playlist" to view your playlist

Troubleshooting: Ensure you're using Chrome or Edge browser. Speak clearly and slowly. If voice not recognized, try with English accent. For Hindi songs, say the name in English.
